My Unfinished Symphony

This digital painting depicts the character Wilbur Soot of the Dream SMP as he delivers his infamous final monologue:

”My great unfinished symphony, forever unfinished. If I can't have this, no one can!”

This was the first drawing I ever completed of Wilbur Soot after becoming interested in the series in early 2021. This single moment has been drawn, painted, animated, and otherwise depicted thousands of times by thousands of other people, and I chose to contribute my own.
