Hello, I'm Molly!

I am a third-year student at Illinois State University studying to earn my Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Graphic Design.

During my time at ISU, I have completed over fifteen courses in the arts and was an active member of the Honors College for four consecutive semesters, all while maintaining a 4.0 GPA.

In my sophomore year, I submitted a portfolio demonstrating my best undergraduate work to be reviewed for admission into the Graphic Design Bachelor of Fine Arts competitive degree program and was accepted in March 2021. I have currently completed four courses exclusive to this degree program as of the conclusion of the Fall 2021 semester, and am on track to graduate in May 2023.
