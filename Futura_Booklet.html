Futura Typeface Specimen

This booklet showcases my research into the popular typeface Futura, examining the anatomy of the typeface itself, the history of the font and the designer Paul Renner, and a number of famous brands that have used Futura in their logos.

I chose to utilize a space-like, science fiction aesthetic in reference to the notable history of Futura in the United States. Not only was it the first typeface to land on the moon, but it has also been used in advertising for several space-related films- notably Stanley Kubrick's 2001: A Space Odyssey- forever linking space and discovery to this typeface.


November 2020
Adobe InDesign and Illustrator
