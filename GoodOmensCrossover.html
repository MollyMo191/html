Good Omens Crossover

This piece combines two of my top favorite pieces of media: the television show Good Omens and the Dream SMP online series. The characters depicted are Ranboo and Tubbo, dressed in the clothing of the two main Good Omens characters Anthony Crowley and Aziraphale, respectively.

Although this piece took far longer to complete than I anticipated, I was very excited to render out the wings on either character. I also chose to mimic the color palette used in Good Omens advertising for the background and details.
